Purpose

Build a Bar was created to help aspiring powerlifters reach new peaks, here at Build A Bar we have trainers, coaches, and doctors ready to help filter in useful information and movements and block out dangerous exercises, harmful foods and bad programs. Here we have everything from free programs, streching routines, diet advice and tips to paid online trainers, diet coaches, and injury specialists to give tips to recover faster and lift more

Audience

The Audience that I would be trying to bring in would be beginner to intermediate powerlifters who want direction and help with their training. This could be advertized to several groups due to the ability that you can use the free version or pay for more elite help with your progress

Content

Home page

Build A Bar Here at Build A Bar, we have the goal to not only help you reach your powerlifting goals but set new ones in record time. No matter your age, weight, or height we promise to have something that will help you in your training. Getting fit and pushing you’re limits can be challenging but that is why we are here, we want to act as a a guide in your progress whether you are just starting out or starting back up again due to injury or old age. We believe that powerlifting is a sport that anyone can try, with this website you can use it to lose weight, increase totals, or simply find a new hobby to help take your mind off of the daily stresses of our lives The main reason why this website was created was because let's all face it, the internet is full of information, some of it is worth looking at that will help you reach your goal, and some of it that is simply false and is going to lead you down a trail of doing useless exercises that help with nothing or is all-out dangerous to do. Luckily here at Build A Bar, we have teams that analyze everything to do with powerlifting. From their analyzations they check if it safe and worthwhile if it is Great, they bring it onto the site so it can be found and used by newcomers to the powerlifting world who are in need of guidance. If it is not safe or worthwhile, they simply don’t add it on to the site

When coming to this you will be able to browse through archives of trainer-approved techniques to help you avoid injury, stretches both before and after your workouts, diet plans that have been used in the past and shown promising results, and most importantly workout plans both free and some with a charge if you are willing to pay a fee, both of which have been looked at with personal trainers and be able to help others in the past increase the total lift weights. With all this data that has been collected you have the ability to fine-tune your programs and diet plan to fit you daily routine and lifestyle. After reading this you are probably thinking to yourself that this is too good to be true, well its not. We want to give knowledge and understanding to all who want to progress with training. We offer both free access to Build A Bar and a paid membership for those looking to go the extra mile with their progress. The paid membership not only gives you the ability to have access to the content that we have found on the web but our team will customize it for you. From giving you advice on programs that will best fit your needs and timeframe to helping you find ways to get your nutrition on track to better perform at the gym as well as in competitions. Payments can be made for different amounts of time, with there being the following packages: one week, one month, three months, six months, and one year. Note that the longer you are subscribed the better the deal is for you.
